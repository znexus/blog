Tokyo, Japan - Chapter Four of the Dreamers Project marked the making of many new friends and the discovery of many new dreams. From presenting our project at PechaKucha on our first night, to finding kindred spirits at the Design Festa Gallery Harajuku, discovering the beautiful Ginko forest for our shoot location, and lingering with the participating dreamers long after the portraits had been taken, it was an intimate and inspiring Chapter for all involved.

Art Block Party for the Bushwick Art Park

Bushwick, NY - Artists and onlookers convened at Vandervoort Place in Bushwick, Brooklyn for the All City Block Party, a day of live painting, DJs, and Calexico tacos. The party marked the beginning of Chapter One of the Bushwick Art Park, Factory Fresh's project with Skewville and Trust Art to convert Vandervoort Place, an underused and garbage-strewn street in Bushwick, into a permanent community art park. At the event, Trust Art obtained over 60 petition signatures in support of the park.

BROOKLYN, NY - In Greenpoint's McGolrick Park a fountain has been installed that emits an all-natural fragrance which carries across much of the park's landscape. The fountain is the work of public artist Anne McClain and a team of five other Brooklyn-based artists. The project was inspired by the question:

Can compassion be transmitted with scent?

A volunteer trip to Mexico in the summer of 2009 was the first chapter of the project, which sought to manifest a scent in public space that inspired everyday acts of compassion.

Thus began the collaboration with Lance McGregor, Alan Iwamura, Isaac Tecosky, Facundo Newbery, Jarek Klim, the New York City Parks Dept, OSA North Brooklyn, St Nicks Alliance, and Robertet.

Greenpoint Artist Greens the Humanity Fountain

Brooklyn, NY - Jarik Klim believes in green art. In the month of the fountain's building, Mr. Klim brought his long-standing relationship with McGolrick park to bear on the project, using leaves from the site of the fountain to cast the white concrete panel that carries the natural fragrance.
